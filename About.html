About Grand Marino Resort

Grand Marino is a countryside haven resort located at Brgy. Candual, Panay, Capiz. Owned by Jonie Vido, this exclusive resort offers top-notch facilities including full-time services and accommodations, making it perfect for all types of gatherings. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, a family retreat, or simply a relaxing vacation surrounded by the beauty of nature, Grand Marino ensures a comfortable and memorable experience.

Just an hour's drive from the city of Roxas, Capiz, our resort offers a unique tropical escape that is both affordable and meets the highest standards of relaxation. Here, guests can enjoy the serenity of nature, combined with modern amenities to make your stay as enjoyable and refreshing as possible.
